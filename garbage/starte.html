Our Services

Residential Collection

Weekly curbside pickup of household garbage for single-family homes and small apartment buildings.

  • Standard 96-gallon bins provided
  • Additional bins available
  • Backdoor service for seniors/disabled

Recycling Services

Comprehensive recycling program for paper, cardboard, glass, metal, and approved plastics.

  • Single-stream recycling (no sorting)
  • 64-gallon bins provided
  • Bi-weekly collection

Yard Waste Collection

Seasonal collection of leaves, grass clippings, branches, and other organic yard waste.

  • March-November collection
  • Paper bags or open containers required
  • Branches must be bundled

Bulk Item Pickup

Scheduled collection of large items that don't fit in standard garbage bins.

  • By appointment only
  • Limit 3 items per pickup
  • Additional fees may apply

Hazardous Waste Disposal

Safe disposal options for household hazardous materials.

  • Monthly drop-off events
  • Accepts paint, chemicals, batteries
  • Proper handling required

Composting Program

Turn your food scraps into nutrient-rich compost for gardens and landscaping.

  • Free compost bins available
  • Workshops and guides
  • Discount on finished compost

Holiday Schedule

When a holiday falls on your collection day, pickup will occur one day later that week.

  • New Year's Day
  • Memorial Day
  • Independence Day
  • Labor Day
  • Thanksgiving Day
  • Christmas Day

No collection on these holidays - service resumes the following day.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time should I put out my garbage? +

All containers should be placed at the curb by 7:00 AM on your collection day, but no earlier than 6:00 PM the evening before. This helps keep our neighborhoods clean and prevents animals from scattering trash.

What items are not accepted in regular garbage? +

The following items cannot be placed in your regular garbage:

  • Hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, batteries)
  • Electronics (TVs, computers)
  • Large appliances
  • Construction debris
  • Medical waste
  • Yard waste (during non-collection months)

Please use our special collection services for these items.

How do I request an additional garbage bin? +

Additional 96-gallon bins are available for an annual fee of $75. You can request one:

  • Online through our service portal
  • By calling our customer service line at (555) 123-4567
  • In person at the Public Works office

Bins are typically delivered within 5 business days of request.

What should I do if my collection was missed? +

If your garbage wasn't collected by 5:00 PM on your scheduled day:

  1. Check our website or social media for service alerts
  2. Ensure your bin was properly placed at the curb
  3. Make sure it wasn't tagged for contamination
  4. If none of these apply, report the missed collection online or by phone

We will arrange for collection within 24 hours of reporting.

How do I dispose of old electronics? +

Electronics recycling is available through several options:

  • Drop-off: Bring items to the city recycling center Tuesday-Saturday 8AM-4PM
  • Special Collection: Schedule a pickup for large items (fee may apply)
  • Retail Takeback: Many electronics stores accept old devices for recycling

There is no charge for most residential electronics recycling.
